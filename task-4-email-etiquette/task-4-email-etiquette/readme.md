In this task, I understood how important professional communication is on digital platforms.

Even if your intention is right, a poorly written email can leave a bad impression.

For example, not adding a proper subject line or using very informal language can make the message seem careless or disrespectful.

By practicing structured emails, I learned that tone, clarity, and proper format really matter.

A clear subject, polite greeting, and respectful closing make the email much more effective and professional.

Overall, this task made me realize that digital communication is not just about sharing information, but also about presenting it in a clear and professional way.
