# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone
- Always check website URLs before entering sensitive information
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online payments
- Regularly update apps and devices
- Be cautious of urgent messages asking for personal information
- For financial fraud, immediately report to cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number 1930 for quick assistance
