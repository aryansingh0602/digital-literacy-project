For Task 1, I created an infographic on digital literacy using Canva. The main objective of this task was to present the concept of digital literacy in a simple, clear, and visually appealing manner. The infographic included essential points such as the meaning of digital literacy, safe internet practices, maintaining a professional online presence, and responsible use of digital platforms. It also highlighted why digital literacy is important in our daily lives.

While designing the infographic, one difficulty I faced was balancing the amount of information with the available space. I had to ensure that the content was informative but not overcrowded, so that it remained easy to read and understand.

This task helped me realize that digital literacy is not only about using technology, but also about using it in a safe, responsible, and effective way.
